RIFA Volunteer

Where: 133 Airway Boulevard, Jackson, TN
When: varies based on opportunity chosen

Volunteer opportunities are available for everyone including individuals, families, corporate groups, civic groups, church groups, school groups and others.
Simply fill out the volunteer application or call 731-427-7963 to set up a time to bring in your application.

Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Sorting food
  • Stocking shelves
  • Assembling snack backpacks or food sacks
  • Sorting clothing
  • Serving in the soup kitchen
  • Baking desserts for the soup kitchen
  • Food donation pick ups
  • Tutoring/mentoring
  • Thrift store clerk
  • Clerical/receptionist duties
